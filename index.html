Overview

Great progress has been achieved in human sensing, understanding and synthesis. We further identify three key issues of the future directions:

  1. We should take a holistic view on the integral pipeline of these topics and explore the frontier problems emerged from their intersections.
  2. The lessons and practices from both academia and industry should be shared and discussed together.
  3. These topics lay the foundation for human-centric AI and will play a greater role in the age of intelligent well-being.
We hope this workshop can foster the inter-disciplinary research in these fields.
